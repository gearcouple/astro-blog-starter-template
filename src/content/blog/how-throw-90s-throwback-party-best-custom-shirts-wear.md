## Why Custom Shirts Make the Party

Here's what I didn't expect: when everyone showed up wearing their custom vintage tees, it instantly broke the ice. Like, you're not just wearing matching shirts—you're wearing *memories*. Photos from high school, embarrassing haircuts, your first car... it all becomes part of the fun. Plus, they double as party favors that people will actually keep (unlike those random trinkets that end up in a drawer somewhere).

The beauty of these shirts is they nail that 90s bootleg aesthetic without looking like cheap knockoffs. You know that vintage rap tee vibe? The slightly faded colors, the bold graphics, the "I definitely got this at a concert" energy? That's exactly what these capture.

## FAQ

**How far in advance should I order custom shirts for a 90s throwback party?**

Give yourself at least 2-3 weeks to be safe. Most take 5-7 business days for production, plus shipping time. If you're doing a group order, add extra time to collect photos from everyone.

**What's the best way to get photos from guests for custom shirts?**

Create a shared Google Drive folder or use a group chat where everyone can upload their favorite 90s-era photos. Make it clear what decade you want—peak awkward years are usually the funniest.

**Can these shirts work for themes other than 90s parties?**

Absolutely! The vintage aesthetic works great for any throwback theme—80s, 70s, even 2000s parties. Just adjust your photo selection and customization text accordingly.

**What size should I order for the oversized vintage look?**

Size up one or two sizes from your normal fit. The baggy, oversized tee look was huge in the 90s, and it's way more comfortable for dancing all night.

**How do I care for custom photo shirts?**

Turn them inside out before washing, use cold water, and skip the dryer if you wanna keep that print looking fresh. Hang dry is your friend here.

**What's the best photo quality to use?**

Higher resolution is always better, but even older scanned photos work well because the vintage treatment actually hides imperfections. Embrace the grainy, nostalgic look!
